Template For Literature Analysis

  • 2025-06-26

Basic Information

This section contains the basic information of the paper, usually used for index and citation purposes.

  • Title and subtitle of paper

  • Author of paper

  • Year of publication

  • Location and date of conference

  • Place of publication (and publisher for books)

  • Category or field of research

  • Other tags

    Note-taking

This section contains note-taking while reading the paper. It could be information extracted from the text, or other thoughts and opinions about the content.

Might include:

  • particular terminologies

  • summary of methodology

  • Inspiring ideas

  • questions that might help to dig further

Analysis

This section mainly focuses on the contents of the paper. It is the summarize and analysis of the articleâ€™s structure, aim, main idea, methodology, theoretical framework, conclusion, and application.

Structure

  • research design (e.g. longitudinal, empirical research)

  • research method (e.g. interview)

  • research focus (e.g. tracking on emotional changes of the subjects in six months)

  • etc.

Aim

This paragraph focuses on the aim of the study.

Main Idea

e.g. how cognitive bias significantly increases depressive symptoms, and what the underlying mechanisms are.

Methodology

  • Participants

  • Research method and design

  • Analytical method

Theoretical Framework

This paragraph focuses on the theoretical framework (and how it is applied to the research) of the study.

Conclusion

  • Relationship between variables

  • Implications

  • Further discussions

  • etc.

Application

This paragraph focuses on the potential application of the study in real life.

Further Research

This section contains deeper research that focuses not only on the paper itself, but also relevant information in the broad topic.

  • Definition for terminologies
  • Explanation of methodologies
  • Relevant theories and ideas
  • Relevant research
  • Answers to the questions during note-taking
  • Reflections
